Belgian Waffles

Ingredients

  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 2 tablespoons sugar
  • 1 tablespoon baking powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1 3/4 cups milk
  • 2 eggs
  • 1/2 cup melted butter
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract

Nutrition Facts

Calories: 350 kcal per waffle

Fat: 18g

Carbohydrates: 38g

Sugar: 5g

Protein: 8g

Sodium: 320mg

Cooking Steps

Step 1Preheat your waffle iron.
Step 2In a bowl, mix flour, sugar, baking powder, and salt.
Step 3In another bowl, whisk milk, eggs, butter, and vanilla.
Step 4Combine wet and dry ingredients. Stir until smooth.
Step 5Pour batter into preheated waffle iron and cook until golden.
Step 6Serve warm with toppings like maple syrup, berries, or whipped cream.
