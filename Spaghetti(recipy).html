Spaghetti alla Carbonara

Ingredients

  • 100g pancetta
  • 50g pecorino cheese
  • 50g parmesan
  • 3 large eggs
  • 350g spaghetti
  • 2 plump garlic cloves, peeled and left whole
  • 50g unsalted butter
  • Sea salt and freshly ground black pepper

Nutrition Facts

Cooking Method

Step 1Put a large saucepan of water on to boil.
Step 2Finely chop the pancetta, remove rind. Grate cheeses and mix.
Step 3Beat the eggs, season, and set aside.
Step 4Add salt to boiling water and cook spaghetti until al dente.
Step 5Squash garlic cloves to bruise.
Step 6Melt butter and fry pancetta and garlic.
Step 7Cook until pancetta is golden. Remove garlic.
Step 8Add spaghetti to pancetta. Don’t drain water completely.
Step 9Mix most cheese with eggs. Keep some cheese for garnish.
Step 10Remove pan from heat, pour in egg-cheese mix, and toss.
Step 11Add pasta water to keep it saucy. Season lightly.
Step 12Twist onto plate, top with cheese and pepper. Serve hot.
