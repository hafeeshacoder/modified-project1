Hyderabadi Chicken Biryani

Ingredients

  • 2 cups basmati rice (soaked for 30 minutes)
  • 500g chicken (marinated)
  • 1 cup yogurt
  • 2 onions (thinly sliced & fried)
  • 1 tbsp ginger-garlic paste
  • 1/2 tsp turmeric powder
  • 1 tsp red chili powder
  • 1/2 tsp garam masala
  • Fresh mint & coriander leaves
  • Saffron soaked in warm milk
  • Salt and oil as required

Nutrition Facts

Calories: 525 kcal per serving

Fat: 18g

Carbohydrates: 65g

Protein: 28g

Sodium: 730mg

Fiber: 3g

Sugar: 5g

Cooking Method

Step 1Marinate chicken with yogurt, spices, and herbs for 1 hour.
Step 2Cook soaked rice until 70% done and drain.
Step 3In a heavy pot, layer marinated chicken, then rice.
Step 4Top with fried onions, saffron milk, and ghee.
Step 5Cover tightly and cook on low flame (dum) for 25 minutes.
Step 6Let it rest, then gently fluff and serve hot with raita.
