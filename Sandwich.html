Grilled Cheese Sandwich

Ingredients

  • 2 slices of bread
  • 2 tablespoons butter
  • 2 slices cheddar cheese
  • Optional: tomato slices, ham, or herbs

Nutrition Facts

Calories: 390 kcal

Fat: 22g

Carbohydrates: 30g

Protein: 13g

Sodium: 620mg

Fiber: 2g

Cooking Steps

Step 1Butter one side of each slice of bread.
Step 2Place cheese between unbuttered sides of bread.
Step 3Heat a skillet over medium heat.
Step 4Place the sandwich on the skillet, buttered side down.
Step 5Grill until golden brown on both sides and cheese melts.
Step 6Slice in half and serve warm.
