Pani Puri (Golgappa)

Ingredients

  • 20 puris (store-bought or homemade)
  • 1 cup boiled and mashed potatoes
  • 1/2 cup boiled moong sprouts or chickpeas
  • 1/2 cup tamarind chutney
  • 1/2 cup finely chopped onions (optional)
  • Chaat masala and black salt to taste
  • For Pani:
  • 1 cup mint leaves
  • 1/2 cup coriander leaves
  • 1 green chili
  • 1 tsp roasted cumin powder
  • 1 tsp tamarind paste
  • Salt to taste and cold water as needed

Nutrition Facts (Per 6 Puris)

Calories: 180 kcal

Fat: 5g

Carbohydrates: 28g

Protein: 4g

Fiber: 3g

Sodium: 350mg

Cooking Steps

Step 1Blend mint, coriander, chili, tamarind, and spices to make pani. Add cold water.
Step 2Mix mashed potatoes and sprouts with chaat masala and black salt.
Step 3Crack open the puris gently on top.
Step 4Fill each puri with potato-sprout mix and chopped onions.
Step 5Add tamarind chutney as desired.
Step 6Dip filled puris in spiced pani and serve immediately.
