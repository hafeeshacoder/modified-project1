Chicken Curry

Ingredients

  • 500g chicken (bone-in or boneless), cut into pieces
  • 2 onions, finely chopped
  • 2 tomatoes, chopped
  • 1/2 cup yogurt
  • 2 tbsp oil or ghee
  • 1 tbsp ginger-garlic paste
  • 2 green chilies, slit
  • 1/2 tsp turmeric powder
  • 1 tsp red chili powder
  • 1 tsp coriander powder
  • 1/2 tsp cumin powder
  • 1 tsp garam masala
  • Salt to taste
  • Fresh coriander leaves for garnish

Nutrition Facts (approximate per serving)

Calories: 390 kcal

Fat: 25g

Carbohydrates: 10g

Protein: 30g

Sodium: 550mg

Cooking Method

Step 1Heat oil in a pan and sauté onions until golden brown.
Step 2Add ginger-garlic paste and green chilies. Sauté for a minute.
Step 3Add chopped tomatoes and cook until soft.
Step 4Add turmeric, chili powder, coriander, cumin, and salt. Mix well.
Step 5Stir in yogurt and cook until the oil starts to separate.
Step 6Add chicken pieces and mix until well coated with masala.
Step 7Cover and cook on low heat for 20–25 minutes until chicken is tender.
Step 8Sprinkle garam masala, garnish with coriander, and serve hot with rice or roti.
