Channa Masala

Ingredients

  • 2 cups cooked chickpeas (channa)
  • 2 tbsp oil
  • 1 large onion, finely chopped
  • 2 tomatoes, chopped
  • 1 tsp ginger-garlic paste
  • 1 green chili, chopped
  • 1 tsp cumin seeds
  • 1/2 tsp turmeric powder
  • 1 tsp coriander powder
  • 1 tsp garam masala
  • 1 tsp channa masala powder
  • Salt to taste
  • Fresh coriander leaves for garnish

Nutrition Facts (per serving)

Calories: 210 kcal

Fat: 7g

Saturates: 1g

Carbohydrates: 28g

Sugars: 4g

Fibre: 6g

Protein: 9g

Salt: 0.8g

Cooking Method

Step 1Heat oil in a pan, add cumin seeds and let them splutter.
Step 2Add onions and sauté until golden brown.
Step 3Add ginger-garlic paste and green chili. Sauté for a minute.
Step 4Add tomatoes and cook until soft.
Step 5Add turmeric, coriander, garam masala, and channa masala powder. Mix well.
Step 6Add chickpeas and 1/2 cup water. Simmer for 10 minutes.
Step 7Garnish with coriander leaves. Serve hot with rice or roti.
