Egg Curry

Ingredients

  • 4 boiled eggs
  • 2 onions, finely chopped
  • 2 tomatoes, pureed
  • 2 green chilies, slit
  • 1 tbsp ginger-garlic paste
  • 1/2 tsp turmeric powder
  • 1 tsp red chili powder
  • 1 tsp garam masala
  • 1/2 tsp cumin seeds
  • Salt to taste
  • 2 tbsp oil
  • Fresh coriander for garnish

Nutrition Facts (approximate per serving)

Calories: 250 kcal

Fat: 18g

Carbohydrates: 7g

Protein: 12g

Cholesterol: 200mg

Cooking Method

Step 1Heat oil in a pan and add cumin seeds. Let them splutter.
Step 2Add onions and sauté until golden brown.
Step 3Add ginger-garlic paste and green chilies. Sauté for 2 minutes.
Step 4Add turmeric, red chili powder, and mix well.
Step 5Add tomato puree and cook until oil separates.
Step 6Add salt, garam masala and a little water for gravy.
Step 7Gently place boiled eggs in the curry. Simmer for 5–7 minutes.
Step 8Garnish with coriander and serve hot with roti or rice.

Frequently Asked Questions

Traditionally, boiled eggs are used. Raw eggs can be poached in the gravy but texture will differ.

You can refrigerate it for up to 2 days. Reheat before serving.
