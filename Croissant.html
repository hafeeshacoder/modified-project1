Classic French Croissant

Ingredients

  • 4 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1/2 cup sugar
  • 1 tbsp instant yeast
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 1 1/4 cups warm milk
  • 300g cold unsalted butter
  • 1 egg (for egg wash)

Nutrition Facts

Calories: 272 kcal per croissant

Fat: 14g

Saturated Fat: 9g

Carbohydrates: 30g

Protein: 5g

Sodium: 210mg

Sugar: 6g

Baking Method

Step 1Mix flour, sugar, salt, and yeast. Add warm milk to form dough.
Step 2Roll dough into a rectangle. Chill.
Step 3Place cold butter slab in center, fold dough over, and roll out.
Step 4Perform 3 letter-folds, chilling between each turn.
Step 5Roll dough out and cut into triangles. Roll each into crescent shape.
Step 6Proof croissants until doubled in size.
Step 7Brush with egg wash. Bake at 200°C (392°F) for 15–20 minutes.
