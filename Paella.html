Spanish Paella

Ingredients

  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 3 garlic cloves, minced
  • 1 red bell pepper, chopped
  • 200g chicken thighs, diced
  • 150g chorizo, sliced
  • 200g raw shrimp, peeled and deveined
  • 1 1/2 cups bomba or arborio rice
  • 1 tsp smoked paprika
  • 1 pinch saffron threads
  • 4 cups chicken broth
  • 1 cup frozen peas
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Lemon wedges for garnish

Nutrition Facts

Calories: 480 kcal per serving

Fat: 20g

Carbohydrates: 45g

Protein: 30g

Sodium: 600mg

Fiber: 5g

Cooking Method

Step 1Heat olive oil in a large paella pan or skillet over medium heat.
Step 2Add onion, garlic, and bell pepper; sauté until softened.
Step 3Add chicken and chorizo; cook until browned.
Step 4Add rice, smoked paprika, and saffron; stir to coat rice in oil.
Step 5Pour in chicken broth; bring to a simmer, then reduce heat to medium-low.
Step 6Cook uncovered for 15 minutes; avoid stirring.
Step 7Add shrimp and peas on top; cook for 5-7 more minutes until rice is tender and shrimp cooked.
Step 8Remove from heat, cover with foil; rest 5 minutes.
Step 9Garnish with lemon wedges and serve hot.
