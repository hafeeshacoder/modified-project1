Margherita Pizza

Ingredients

  • 1 pizza base (12-inch)
  • 1/2 cup pizza sauce
  • 1 cup fresh mozzarella cheese
  • Fresh basil leaves
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Nutrition Facts

Calories: 270 kcal per slice

Fat: 10g

Carbohydrates: 32g

Protein: 12g

Sodium: 480mg

Calcium: 15% DV

Cooking Steps

Step 1Preheat your oven to 230°C (450°F).
Step 2Spread pizza sauce evenly over the base.
Step 3Add slices of mozzarella evenly across the pizza.
Step 4Sprinkle with salt and pepper, and drizzle olive oil.
Step 5Bake for 10–12 minutes until crust is golden and cheese is bubbly.
Step 6Remove from oven, top with fresh basil leaves, and serve hot.
