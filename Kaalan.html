Kaalan

Ingredients

  • 400g raw banana (peeled and chopped)
  • 200g yam (peeled and chopped)
  • 1 cup thick yogurt
  • 1/4 cup grated coconut
  • 2 green chilies
  • 1 tsp turmeric powder
  • 1 tsp mustard seeds
  • 2 dried red chilies
  • A few curry leaves
  • Salt to taste
  • 2 tbsp coconut oil

Nutrition Facts (approximate per serving)

Calories: 180 kcal

Fat: 9g

Saturates: 7g

Carbohydrates: 20g

Protein: 3g

Fiber: 5g

Sodium: 350mg

Cooking Method

Step 1Cook chopped raw banana and yam in salted water until soft. Drain and set aside.
Step 2Grind grated coconut, green chilies, and a little water to a smooth paste.
Step 3Whisk yogurt until smooth.
Step 4Mix the cooked vegetables with the coconut paste, turmeric powder, salt, and yogurt in a pot.
Step 5Cook on medium heat stirring gently until it thickens, but don’t let it boil too hard to avoid curdling.
Step 6Heat coconut oil in a small pan, add mustard seeds, dried red chilies, and curry leaves. When mustard seeds splutter, pour this tempering over the kaalan.
Step 7Mix gently and serve hot with rice.
