Classic Greek Salad

Ingredients

  • 3 large tomatoes, chopped
  • 1 cucumber, sliced
  • 1 green bell pepper, sliced
  • 1 small red onion, thinly sliced
  • 200g feta cheese, cubed
  • 20 black olives (Kalamata preferred)
  • 3 tbsp extra virgin olive oil
  • 1 tbsp red wine vinegar
  • 1 tsp dried oregano
  • Salt and freshly ground black pepper to taste

Nutrition Facts

Calories: 250 kcal per serving

Fat: 20g

Carbohydrates: 8g

Protein: 6g

Sodium: 580mg

Fiber: 2g

Preparation Steps

Step 1In a large bowl, combine tomatoes, cucumber, bell pepper, and red onion.
Step 2Add black olives and cubed feta cheese on top.
Step 3In a small bowl, whisk olive oil, red wine vinegar, oregano, salt, and pepper.
Step 4Pour dressing over the salad and toss gently.
Step 5Serve immediately with crusty bread or as a side dish.

Frequently Asked Questions

Traditional Greek salad uses feta cheese made from sheep’s milk or a mix of sheep and goat’s milk.

Classic Greek salad does not include lettuce, but some variations add it for extra crunch.

Best served fresh but can be refrigerated for up to 24 hours. Tomatoes may release water over time.
