Cheese Omelette

Ingredients

  • 2 eggs
  • 1/4 cup grated cheddar or mozzarella cheese
  • 1 tbsp milk (optional, for fluffiness)
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 1 tbsp butter or oil
  • Optional: chopped onions, tomatoes, green chilies

Nutrition Facts (per omelette)

Calories: 220 kcal

Fat: 18g

Carbohydrates: 1.5g

Protein: 12g

Cholesterol: 180mg

Cooking Method

Step 1Crack the eggs into a bowl, add salt, pepper, and milk. Beat well.
Step 2Heat butter in a non-stick pan over medium heat.
Step 3Pour the egg mixture into the pan and let it cook without stirring.
Step 4Sprinkle cheese evenly on the semi-cooked omelette.
Step 5Once the bottom is firm, fold the omelette in half and cook for another minute.
Step 6Slide onto a plate and serve hot with toast or salad.
