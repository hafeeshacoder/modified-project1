Authentic Pad Thai

Ingredients

  • 200g flat rice noodles
  • 150g shrimp or chicken
  • 2 tbsp vegetable oil
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 2 eggs
  • 1 cup bean sprouts
  • 3 tbsp Pad Thai sauce (or tamarind paste + sugar + fish sauce)
  • 1/4 cup chopped peanuts
  • 2 green onions, chopped
  • Lime wedges for serving

Nutrition Facts

Calories: 450 kcal per serving

Fat: 14g

Carbohydrates: 60g

Protein: 20g

Sodium: 850mg

Fiber: 3g

Sugar: 8g

Cooking Method

Step 1Soak rice noodles in warm water until soft, about 30 minutes.
Step 2In a wok, heat oil and sauté garlic. Add shrimp or chicken.
Step 3Push protein aside, crack in eggs, and scramble.
Step 4Add drained noodles and Pad Thai sauce. Stir-fry well.
Step 5Add bean sprouts, peanuts, and green onions.
Step 6Stir everything together. Serve hot with lime wedges.
