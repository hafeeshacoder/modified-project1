Chicken Shawarma

Ingredients

  • 500g boneless chicken thighs
  • 2 tbsp plain yogurt
  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 tsp cumin powder
  • 1 tsp paprika
  • 1/2 tsp turmeric
  • 1/2 tsp cinnamon
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Pita bread or flatbread
  • Lettuce, tomato, onion for filling
  • Garlic sauce or tahini (optional)

Nutrition Facts

Calories: 450 kcal

Fat: 23g

Carbohydrates: 30g

Protein: 35g

Sodium: 480mg

Fiber: 2g

Cooking Steps

Step 1Mix yogurt, olive oil, and spices in a bowl to create a marinade.
Step 2Add chicken to the marinade, coat well, and let it rest for 2 hours or overnight.
Step 3Grill or pan-fry the chicken until fully cooked and slightly charred.
Step 4Slice the chicken into strips.
Step 5Warm pita or flatbread, and fill with chicken and toppings of choice.
Step 6Add garlic sauce or tahini, wrap tightly, and serve warm.
