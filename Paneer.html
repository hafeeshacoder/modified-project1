Paneer Butter Masala

Ingredients

  • 250g paneer, cubed
  • 3 tbsp butter
  • 2 medium onions, finely chopped
  • 2 large tomatoes, pureed
  • 1 tbsp ginger-garlic paste
  • 1/2 cup fresh cream
  • 1 tsp red chili powder
  • 1 tsp garam masala
  • 1 tsp kasuri methi (dried fenugreek leaves)
  • Salt to taste
  • Fresh coriander leaves for garnish
  • 1 tbsp oil

Nutrition Facts (approximate per serving)

Calories: 350 kcal

Fat: 28g

Carbohydrates: 10g

Protein: 14g

Fiber: 2g

Sodium: 400mg

Cooking Method

Step 1Heat oil and 2 tbsp butter in a pan. Add chopped onions and sauté till golden.
Step 2Add ginger-garlic paste and sauté for 2 minutes until raw smell disappears.
Step 3Add tomato puree, red chili powder, and salt. Cook till oil separates from the masala.
Step 4Add garam masala and kasuri methi. Mix well.
Step 5Add paneer cubes and mix gently to coat with the gravy.
Step 6Add fresh cream and remaining butter. Simmer for 5 minutes on low heat.
Step 7Garnish with chopped coriander leaves and serve hot with naan or rice.
