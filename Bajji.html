Bajji

Ingredients

  • 1 cup gram flour (besan)
  • 1 tsp rice flour (for crispiness)
  • 1/2 tsp turmeric powder
  • 1 tsp red chili powder
  • Salt to taste
  • Water as needed to make batter
  • Vegetables for coating (banana peppers, potatoes, onions, raw mango)
  • Oil for deep frying

Nutrition Facts (approximate per serving)

Calories: 220 kcal

Carbohydrates: 30g

Fat: 10g

Protein: 5g

Fiber: 3g

Sodium: 250mg

Cooking Method

Step 1Mix gram flour, rice flour, turmeric, chili powder, salt in a bowl.
Step 2Add water gradually to make a thick batter.
Step 3Heat oil in a deep frying pan on medium heat.
Step 4Slice vegetables as desired (e.g., banana peppers into long pieces).
Step 5Dip each vegetable piece into batter, coating well.
Step 6Carefully drop into hot oil and fry till golden and crisp.
Step 7Remove and drain on paper towels.
Step 8Serve hot with chutney or ketchup.

Frequently Asked Questions

Gram flour is best for authentic taste and texture. All-purpose flour will change the flavor.

Adding a little rice flour and frying at medium heat helps make bajjis crispy.
