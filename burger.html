Classic Cheeseburger

Ingredients

  • 500g ground beef (80% lean)
  • Salt and freshly ground black pepper
  • 4 burger buns
  • 4 slices cheddar cheese
  • Lettuce leaves
  • Tomato slices
  • Red onion rings
  • Pickles, ketchup, mustard, mayonnaise (optional)

Nutrition Facts

Calories: 550 kcal per burger

Fat: 30g

Carbohydrates: 30g

Protein: 35g

Sodium: 620mg

Fiber: 2g

Cooking Steps

Step 1Preheat a grill or pan to high heat.
Step 2Form ground beef into 4 patties. Season with salt and pepper.
Step 3Grill patties for about 3–4 minutes per side.
Step 4Place a slice of cheese on each patty during the last minute of cooking.
Step 5Toast the buns until golden brown.
Step 6Assemble burgers with lettuce, tomato, onion, pickles, and sauce.
Step 7Serve hot with fries or coleslaw.
