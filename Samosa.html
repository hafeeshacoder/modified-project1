Indian Samosa

Ingredients

  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 4 tbsp oil or ghee
  • Salt to taste
  • Water (as needed for dough)
  • 3 boiled potatoes (mashed)
  • 1/2 cup green peas
  • 1 tsp cumin seeds
  • 1 tsp ginger, grated
  • 1-2 green chilies, chopped
  • 1 tsp garam masala
  • 1/2 tsp amchur (dry mango powder)

Nutrition Facts (1 Samosa)

Calories: 262 kcal

Fat: 17g

Saturates: 3g

Carbohydrates: 22g

Sugars: 2g

Fibre: 3g

Protein: 4g

Salt: 0.6g

Cooking Method

Step 1Mix flour, salt, and oil. Add water to make a firm dough. Let rest 30 mins.
Step 2In a pan, heat oil, add cumin, ginger, and green chilies. Add mashed potatoes, peas, and spices. Mix well.
Step 3Roll dough into circles, cut in half, shape cones, and fill with stuffing.
Step 4Seal the edges with water. Deep fry samosas on medium heat until golden brown.
Step 5Drain excess oil and serve hot with chutney.
