Chocolate Lava Cake

Ingredients

  • 1/2 cup (115g) unsalted butter
  • 170g semi-sweet chocolate (chopped or chips)
  • 1 cup powdered sugar
  • 2 whole eggs
  • 2 egg yolks
  • 6 tbsp all-purpose flour
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract
  • Butter and cocoa powder for ramekins

Nutrition Facts

Calories: 400 kcal per cake

Fat: 26g

Sugar: 24g

Carbohydrates: 35g

Protein: 6g

Sodium: 60mg

Cooking Steps

Step 1Preheat oven to 220°C. Butter and dust ramekins with cocoa powder.
Step 2Melt butter and chocolate together until smooth. Let cool slightly.
Step 3Whisk in powdered sugar, eggs, and egg yolks.
Step 4Stir in flour and vanilla extract until just combined.
Step 5Pour batter into prepared ramekins (¾ full).
Step 6Bake for 12–14 minutes until edges are firm and centers soft.
Step 7Let rest 1 minute, then invert onto plate. Serve immediately!
