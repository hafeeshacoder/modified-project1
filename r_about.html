About Railway Gate Tracker

The Railway Gate Tracker is a user-friendly platform designed to provide real-time updates on the opening and closing status of railway gates across cities and towns. It aims to improve road safety by helping drivers, cyclists, and pedestrians plan their route in advance and avoid waiting near closed gates.

Whether you're commuting to work, transporting goods, or traveling with family, this tracker ensures a smooth journey by showing you the latest gate activity in your area. Integrated with live data and regularly updated, the system provides accurate timing and status so you can travel smarter and safer.

Stay tuned as we expand our coverage to more regions and enhance features like voice alerts, gate history logs, and location-based notifications.
